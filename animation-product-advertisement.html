Project 2 - Product Advertisement

For my second Adobe Animate project, I created a product advertisement for the Apple iPhone designed to appear on the Best Buy website.
The ad features animated iPhone images, headlines, and a call-to-action button that links directly to the product page.
All branding follows Best Buy's style guide, ensuring consistency in colors, logos, and design elements.
