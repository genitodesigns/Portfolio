Project 1 - Photo Restoration

For my first Adobe Photoshop project, I restored an old, damaged photograph from Wikimedia.
The goal was to preserve the image’s original charm while repairing imperfections and maintaining an authentic look.
I worked non-destructively using Smart Objects and a clear layer structure to keep the process organized and editable.
Color correction, clone stamp cleanup, and reconstruction were applied in stages to ensure a seamless, natural result.
Finally, Neural Filters were used for subtle enhancements, improving contrast and removing artifacts without over-editing.
The final image reflects both technical skill and respect for the original photograph, resulting in a clean, professional restoration.
