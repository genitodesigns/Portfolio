Color Theory Article

In this article, I compare the use of color on two different websites. Color is an essential part of visual communication, affecting readability, mood, accessibility, and overall user experience. By examining how each site uses its color palettes, we can better understand why certain choices succeed while others fall short.

Website 1: Apple

Website: https://www.apple.com

Apple's website is well known for its minimalist color palette, typically using black, white, and subtle grays.

Because Apple relies heavily on product photography, their restrained use of color allows each featured device to stand out.

Website 2: Spotify

Website: https://www.spotify.com

In contrast to Apple's minimal palette, Spotify uses bold and vibrant colors throughout its website.

Spotify's color choices also help guide users' attention.

Comparison

The two websites use color very differently to support their goals.
